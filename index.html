About Us

Who is Dream Car Detail

Campbell Wright, Owner and founder of Dream Car Detail, has had a passion for cars long before he had his license. He grew up helping his dad work on old trucks, and has been working on vehicles ever since. Campbell found his passion for auto detailing while maintaining his father’s car collection. He was in charge of maintaining the condition of many cars, including classic trucks and high-end sports cars. He then honed his skills working for Haberman Auto Detail, a local mobile detail business formerly operating in Edmonds. Campbell then went off to college, and ultimately decided that pursuing entrepreneurship in the auto detail industry and providing an excellent, honest service to the Edmonds community was more suited for him. And Dream Car Detail was born! Remember that feeling of bringing home that brand-new car? Well at Dream Car Detail, we want to make your car feel brand new again. We believe that any car can provide an excellent driving experience when it’s cleaned and protected from the elements. We make any car your dream car.
